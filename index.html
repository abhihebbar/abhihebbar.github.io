Who I am

I make things. Products, companies, tools, songs, stories.

I'm drawn to the beginning of things — when the problem is still fuzzy and the solution doesn't exist yet. That's where I do my best thinking. I'm a builder by instinct, a numbers person by necessity, and curious about almost everything by default.

I move between technology, business, and creative work without a clean boundary between them. Films, history, philosophy, music — these aren't hobbies I do after work. They're how I think.

Work

I'm CTO at Lets Flo, a SaaS platform for enterprise fashion and retail. I lead the technology function — architecture, product, engineering — and also take care of the financial side of the business.

I like building systems that make complex operations feel simple. Internal tools, AI pipelines, workflow engines, APIs. The kind of software that quietly does a lot.
